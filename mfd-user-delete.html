Delete Your Data

To remove all your data from MyFinanceDiary, follow these simple steps:

  1. 1 Open the MyFinanceDiary app on your device.
  2. 2 Tap Settings in the bottom navigation.
  3. 3 Tap Delete Data and confirm your choice.
